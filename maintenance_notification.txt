Here is a sample email template for a server maintenance notification:

Subject: Scheduled Server Maintenance

Hello [Name],

We would like to inform you that we will be performing scheduled maintenance on our server on [Date] at [Time]. During this time, our website and any services that are hosted on the server may be unavailable.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause, and we appreciate your patience while we complete this necessary maintenance.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Thank you,
[Your Name]
